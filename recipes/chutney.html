Chutney for the win

Description

chutney is a popular side dish which goes along well with dosa, it is given with dosa at all restaurants and shares a beautiful relationship with Dosa

It is made with coconut, peanut, mustard, curry leaves and chilli and can get real spicy sometimes.

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Peanut
  • chilli
  • mustard
  • curry leaves

Steps

  1. Roast the peanuts and remove seed coat
  2. Grind the peanut, add some water, add coconut, chilli and grind them together
  3. take a small pan and add 2 teaspoons of oil and add mustard and curry leaves to it once the oil is well boiling
  4. pour the oil mustard mix to the grinded chutney and add some salt
  5. Mix and serve with dosa
